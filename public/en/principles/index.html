Principles

At hello world, we operate based on the 4 'P's, the core principles of creative learning according to the Lifelong Kindergarten laboratory at MIT in the United States. At our school, learners develop their ability to learn on their own.

Projects

Creativity is one of the most important skills in programming. Project-based learning enhances creativity and deepens understanding.

Passion

Learners are more focused and engaged in projects that interest them. We work on a variety of projects such as games and animations, focusing on those that interest the learner.

Peers

Learners acquire social communication skills through creativity. They can create while cooperating, collaborating, and sharing with others.

Play

The Lifelong Kindergarten group sees play 'not as an activity, but rather as an attitude: a way of engaging with the world'. Playfulness is described as 'constantly experimenting, trying new things, taking risks, and testing the boundaries'.
