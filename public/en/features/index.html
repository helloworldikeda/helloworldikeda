Programming and English

Programming and English classes in one place. All native English teachers.

English Immersion

All classes are taught in English. By learning something in English, it doesn't feel like studying. Tasks are designed for minimal English ability, so don't worry about your level.

Project-based curriculum

In programming classes, you will set your own goals, work toward them, and learn the tools needed to complete each task.

Children to Adults

The curriculum is structured according to age and level, so everyone from children to adults can attend.

Group and Private Lessons

You can choose to have fun lessons with your classmates in a group or intensive one-on-one learning.

Online Lessons

Both children and adults can choose private lessons in-person or online . Group lessons are held face-to-face at the school.

Simple Pricing

All prices include consumption tax. If you contact us by the day before, you can take makeup lessons within the same month. We accept cash, bank transfers and PayPay.

Learning Technology

Both the programming class and the English class use audio, video, and games to make learning fun. Even beginners can enjoy it.
